A little about my
background and goals

Click here to download pdf version of the statement below.

Born into the internet era where children and youth manage the digital medium and electronic devices with apparent ease in comparison with previous generations. Having dictionaries, maps, phone catalogs, books and seemingly everything that was previously printed on paper available at the touch of a finger made me very interested in the digital world from an early age. As I grew my appreciation for technologies also did. So much, in fact, that when it came time to decide what to study after high school, although intimidated by its complexity, I decided to enroll in a computer science bachelor program. There I was, a spear fisherman that loved Star Wars and whose main priority was to love and respect God, now decided to study computers and computer software.

However, that was four years ago and I’m still working on my degree. With good grades but in all honesty, feeling like I haven’t made the best out of the opportunities that I’ve had. Haven’t been in an internship, haven’t had working experience in the industry and with a limited portfolio of projects. But its time to change and this website is the first step. This will allow me to have a public portfolio where I can change the course of my life. My renewed spirit and new goal driven attitude will help me become a competent professional in the field. My time in university has given me an incredible perspective in how we live as a society. Therefore, I want a job where I can create and develop apps that help our society identify our main problems and possibly even solve them. I believe the digital medium is a tool that has a global reach and the possibilities for positive social impact are endless. Spreading accurate information and statistics, creating apps and networks that allow people to find projects and institutions that work for the benefit of our planet and society, creating platform to help third world countries and much more. If the work at you company/institution aligns with this vision, I’d be glad to work for you and put my skills and passion to the service of our society.

Although I had a slow start, today I am awake and ready to take on the challenges that these times are throwing our way. Through software we can change the world and I’m ready to take part in this amazing field of computer science.



Thank You and I hope we can work toghether soon!
